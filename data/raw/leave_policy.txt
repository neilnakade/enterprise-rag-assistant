Company: TechNova Solutions

Leave Policy:

1. Casual Leave:
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per year.

2. Sick Leave:
Employees get 10 sick leaves annually. Medical proof may be required for more than 2 consecutive days.

3. Paid Leave:
15 paid leaves are provided annually.

4. Public Holidays:
Employees get 10 company-declared holidays per year.

5. Leave Approval:
All leaves must be approved by the reporting manager in advance.
