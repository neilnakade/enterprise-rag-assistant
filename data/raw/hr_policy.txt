Company: TechNova Solutions

HR Policy:

1. Working Hours:
Employees are expected to work 9 hours per day, including a 1-hour break.
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

2. Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and follow ethical practices.

3. Remote Work:
Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval.

4. Dress Code:
Smart casuals are expected in office environments.

5. Attendance:
Regular attendance is mandatory. Repeated absence without notice may lead to disciplinary action.
