Company: TechNova Solutions

Employee Onboarding Guide:

1. First Day:
Employees must report to HR at 9:00 AM.

2. Documents Required:
ID proof, educational certificates, and offer letter.

3. System Setup:
IT team will provide login credentials and system access.

4. Training:
Employees must complete mandatory onboarding training within the first week.

5. Manager Introduction:
New employees will meet their reporting manager on Day 1.
